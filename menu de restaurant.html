Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Maakouda (Fried Potato Patties)

    Delicious potato fritters mixed with spices, herbs, and sometimes stuffed with cheese. These golden patties have a crispy exterior and a soft, flavorful interior.

  • Bourek (Algerian Stuffed Pastry)

    Crispy golden rolls filled with minced meat, onions, and spices, often served during Ramadan or family gatherings. They are perfect as a savory starter.

  • Chlada Felfel (Roasted Bell Pepper Salad)

    A vibrant and refreshing salad made with roasted bell peppers, tomatoes, and onions, dressed with olive oil and lemon juice. It's a perfect appetizer for warm days.

Main Courses

  • Rechta (Algerian Noodles with Chicken)

    Homemade thin noodles served with tender chicken, chickpeas, and a flavorful white sauce infused with spices. Often enjoyed during special celebrations.

  • Tajine Zitoun (Olive and Chicken Stew)

    A savory stew made with chicken, green olives, mushrooms, and a zesty lemon sauce. It is beautifully seasoned and often served with crusty bread.

  • Couscous Royale

    A signature dish of fluffy couscous topped with lamb, chicken, vegetables, and chickpeas, all served in a savory broth. It's a beloved national dish often enjoyed during festive gatherings.

Desserts

  • Makroud (Semolina Date Cookies)

    Golden diamond-shaped cookies made from semolina dough, stuffed with a sweet date filling, and soaked in honey. They have a crispy exterior and a rich, sweet taste.

  • Kalb el Louz (Semolina and Almond Cake)

    A dense semolina cake soaked in orange blossom syrup, filled with almonds, and cut into diamond shapes. It's a classic dessert during Ramadan.

  • Baklawa (Algerian-Style Baklava)

    Layers of thin pastry filled with a fragrant mix of almonds and walnuts, drizzled with honey or syrup. Algerian baklawa is often spiced with cinnamon and rosewater for a unique flavor.
